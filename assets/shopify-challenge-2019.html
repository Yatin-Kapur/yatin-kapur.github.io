As we studied earlier, there is one shop which is charging just way more than the rest of the shops for the sneakers, and there is another shop which is receiving orders for a lot more items than the other shops. This is skewing the data in such a way which doesn't accurately represent the average order value. Now, to decrease the impact of the outliers, there are three options:

  1. take the median of all order_amount in the 'data' dataframe so that all data is accounted for, but outliers are largely ignored
  2. exclude these shops from the dataset and then take AOV from a new dataframe
